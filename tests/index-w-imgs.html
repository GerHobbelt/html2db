The CAT manual-snippets cobbled together (on-line) (1 MB HTML)

This is what I use as an off-line / on-line CAT reference manual, as the official Wiki is lacking as well. And given the rate that is updated, it's easy to track those few edits a year in this document.

The on-line version is here (1MB - takes a while to download), while you can download the archived version for off-net use below (when you have CAT on your laptop, like I do, and not with Net access all the time, while you're at it). Note that you'll need 7zip (available free of charge at www.7-zip.org) to extract the archive.

NOTE: the (v0.7beta + Wiki) CATmuscle documentation is also part of this document.
